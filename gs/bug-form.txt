Before you send in a bug report, please check the list of problems found
after the latest release.  Currently you will find this list at
	http://www.vix.com/ghost/post###.html
where ### is the release number (e.g., 510).

If your problem hasn't been reported yet, please fill out this form and
e-mail it to ghost@aladdin.com.  We can't promise to investigate every
reported problem, but we do give priority to those reported using this form.
It is very important that you fill out *every* applicable entry: in
particular, we can't investigate problem if you don't provide the input
data.  Thanks.
